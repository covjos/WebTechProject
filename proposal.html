Joey Coviello Project Proposal

My website is going to be a news and stats website about the Cleveland Cavaliers. The objective of my website is to give fans of the Cleveland Cavaliers up-to-date news about the team as well as up-to-date stats, game scores, and game information. My target audience are fans of the Cleveland Cavaliers and maybe just fans of basketball in general. I will have a home page of my website that will be a very eye-catching page that will draw people into my website. I will also have appropraie links on this page linking to other pages throughout the website. The sitemap page will also have links to appropriate pages throughout the website. The contact page will have my information and the Cleveland Cavaliers information. The other four webpages will be one about stats of individual players, one about the game schedule, one about game scores, one about the roster of the team, and one about current news for the team.
